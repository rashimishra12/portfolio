Case Studies

Strategic Work

From objective to outcome — every campaign with a story.

01 — PR STRATEGY
Building a 150+ Media Placement Engine from Zero
+
Problem

An interior design firm with strong work but minimal media presence, no journalist relationships, and no systematic PR strategy. Brand recognition was limited to word-of-mouth within existing networks.

Strategy

Developed an integrated PR roadmap spanning media outreach, HARO-based journalist relationship building, podcast pitching, award nominations, and panel speaker positioning — niche trade press first, then national outlets.

Execution

Built a media contact database from scratch. Crafted individualized pitches per journalist vertical. Managed editorial calendars, coordinated interview prep, and produced supporting content for each placement.

Result

150+ placements across Forbes, LA Times, Commercial Observer, Crain's, Construction Executive, and dozens of trade publications — positioning the firm as a top-cited voice in commercial interior design.

02 — SOCIAL MEDIA
30% Follower Growth Through Data-Driven Content Strategy
+
Problem

Social channels were underperforming — low engagement, inconsistent posting, and no measurable connection between content and business outcomes. Follower growth had plateaued across all platforms.

Strategy

Full channel audit and competitive analysis. Identified high-performing content categories and built a 4-week content calendar template with vertical-specific adaptations for each platform.

Execution

Implemented structured content workflows, integrated analytics dashboards, A/B tested caption formats, and aligned social content to concurrent press placements for amplified reach. Produced 200+ pieces.

Result

Average 30% growth in followers and engagement. Posts tied to media coverage consistently outperformed organic content 3–5x. Social now functions as an active brand visibility tool, not just a content archive.

03 — AWARDS STRATEGY
Securing 50+ Awards & Industry Nominations
+
Problem

No systematic awards strategy. Recognition was reactive — relying on unsolicited nominations rather than a managed, proactive submissions process tied to broader PR goals.

Strategy

Researched and mapped 80+ relevant awards across real estate, interior design, construction, and women-in-business categories. Created a rolling awards calendar with deadlines and tailored messaging.

Execution

Drafted compelling submissions, coordinated documentation, managed follow-up, and leveraged each win for concurrent press cycles — turning each award into a PR trigger event.

Result

Recognition from Crain's, GlobeSt., Forbes, City & State NY, AI Magazine, Corporate Vision, BUILD, Connect CRE, and 40+ others — cementing the firm as a nationally recognized design leader.

04 — TEAM & OPERATIONS
Scaling a 12-Member Marketing Team Across 3 Markets
+
Problem

The marketing function was a one-person operation without defined workflows, KPIs, or the team capacity to execute at the volume required to support a growing multi-city firm.

Strategy

Mapped the full marketing and PR function against growth targets for New York, Florida, and Toronto. Designed a team structure with clear role ownership and skills requirements before any hiring began.

Execution

Collaborated with HR on recruitment pipelines, conducted interviews, onboarded hires, and built internal SOPs, tooling standards, and performance review frameworks. Led weekly standups.

Result

Built and managed a high-performing 12-member team. Supported broader hiring that brought 30+ professionals into the design division. Full strategic and executional capacity across all markets.
